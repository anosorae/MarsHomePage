Open Positions

1. Research Assistants, Postdocs, and Visiting Scholars

We have several openings for visiting scholars and research assistants with competitive salaries. Research interests include but not limited to the following areas:

  • Big Data Analytics and Management
  • Graph Database and Social Networks Analysis
  • Data Mining and Visualization
  • Blockchain
  • Crowdsourcing and Distributed Data Management
  • Data Privacy and Security
  • Mobile and Spatial Databases.

Please email me your CV and supporting documents, with the following subject: [RA/Postdoc/Visiting Application] Name+School.

2. PhD Students (招收博士研究生)

I am looking for strong PhD students to compete for Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) 2019/20, please email me your CV and supporting documents, with the following subject: [PhD Application] Name+School.

Qualifications: Students with a bachelor degree in computer science or other related fields are all welcome.

  1. Meet HKBU requirements for English and GPA.
  2. Strongly insterested in database and data mining.
  3. A solid background in mathematics, data structure, and algorithm is a plus.
  4. Experience with ACM/ICPC is a plus.

Please visit details on admission procedure. In addition, undergraduate/master students who will graduate by summer 2020, are encouraged to visit HKBU by applying for 2019 Summer PhD Research Experience Programme.

The Database Research Group is part of the Department of Computer Science. The members of the Group have published more than 200 papers in top conferences (including SIGMOD, VLDB, KDD, ICDE) and prestigious journals (including TODS, TKDE, VLDBJ). In the past few years, the Group has received over $10 million of research grants.
